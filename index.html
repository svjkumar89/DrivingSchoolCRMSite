Frequently asked questions

?

Does it work without internet?

Yes — every screen works fully offline. Pro and Business add cloud sync so your data is backed up and available on multiple devices whenever you're online.

?

Can I import my existing Excel data?

Yes. Export your sheet as CSV and import customers, vehicles and renewals directly — no retyping years of records.

?

Which renewals can I track?

RC, FC, Insurance, PUC and Permit are built in, plus driving licence and LLR expiry per customer — and you can add your own renewal types with custom validity periods.

?

Can my staff have their own logins?

On the Business plan, invite staff by email — they sign in with their own Google account and work in your branch with owner-controlled roles.

?

Is my data safe?

Your data lives on your device, and on paid plans it's additionally backed up to the cloud with per-account isolation. You can export everything to CSV at any time — no lock-in.

?

How do I cancel?

Anytime, in two taps, from your Google Play subscriptions page. Your data stays intact on the Free plan — nothing is deleted.
