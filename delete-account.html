Delete Your Account & Data

App: DrivingSchoolCRM (package com.driveoffice.app)

You can request deletion of your DrivingSchoolCRM account and the data associated with it at any time, whether or not you still have the App installed.

How to request deletion

Email axebelk.apps@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign in, with the subject line "Delete my account." To help us find your account quickly, please include:

  • The email address linked to your Google Sign-In
  • Your driving school / branch name, if you remember it
  • The mobile number on your profile, if different from your email

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile information (name, email, profile photo from Google Sign-In).
  • Your subscription and billing status on our side (your Google Play purchase history remains with Google, governed by their own policies).
  • If you were on the Pro plan: all of your workspace's business data stored in our cloud database — customers, vehicles, renewals, leads, payments, and branch details.
  • Push notification tokens associated with your account.

What we keep, and why

We don't keep a copy of your business data after deletion. We may retain minimal records (such as that a deletion request was made and processed) as needed to prevent abuse and to meet legal obligations. We do not retain your customers' personal data beyond what's needed to complete the deletion itself.

Data stored only on your device (Free plan usage, or any local data on a Pro device) is not something we can remotely delete — uninstalling the App removes it, or you can delete individual records from within the App at any time.

Timeframe

We process deletion requests manually and will complete verified requests within 7 business days. We'll confirm by email once it's done.

Deleting specific data instead of your whole account

If you just want to remove individual customers, vehicles, or other records rather than delete your entire account, you can do that directly inside the App — no need to contact us.

Contact

Questions about this process? Email axebelk.apps@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
